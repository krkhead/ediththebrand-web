# Website Preview Concept Package

## Format

- Length: 30 to 40 seconds
- Orientation: landscape master for presentations, easy to crop into vertical teasers later
- Style: premium, warm, modern, editorial
- Purpose: quickly communicate what the website is, how it feels, and why it is easy to shop

## Core Message

This is a premium skincare storefront built to make discovery feel elegant, shopping feel easy, and checkout feel personal.

## Recommended Edit Structure

1. Brand mood and homepage
2. Collections-led discovery
3. All-products browsing with search and filters
4. Product detail trust and clarity
5. Cart and WhatsApp checkout
6. Soft close with brand confidence

## 35-Second Voiceover Script

### Full Voiceover

\"Discover a skincare storefront designed to feel as refined as the products it presents. From the very first moment, the experience leads you through curated collections, intuitive search, and beautifully organized product pages. Customers can explore by need, read reviews, build their cart with ease, apply offers instantly, and complete their order through a simple WhatsApp handoff. It is premium, clear, and made to help a beauty brand sell with confidence.\"

### Split by Scene

1. \"Discover a skincare storefront designed to feel as refined as the products it presents.\"
2. \"From the very first moment, the experience leads you through curated collections and intuitive discovery.\"
3. \"Customers can browse with search, filters, and beautifully organized product pages.\"
4. \"They can read reviews, build their cart with ease, and apply offers instantly.\"
5. \"And when they are ready, the order moves smoothly into a simple WhatsApp handoff.\"
6. \"It is premium, clear, and made to help a beauty brand sell with confidence.\"

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 - Homepage Arrival

- Duration: 0 to 6 seconds
- Visual: hero section with logo, headline, search bar, and collage imagery
- Camera move: slow push-in
- On-screen text: `A premium skincare storefront`
- Voiceover: \"Discover a skincare storefront designed to feel as refined as the products it presents.\"

### Scene 2 - Collections First

- Duration: 6 to 12 seconds
- Visual: shop landing page with collection cards
- Camera move: gentle pan across collection grid
- On-screen text: `Collections-led discovery`
- Voiceover: \"From the very first moment, the experience leads you through curated collections and intuitive discovery.\"

### Scene 3 - All Products View

- Duration: 12 to 18 seconds
- Visual: all-products page with search, sorting, and category filters visible
- Camera move: slight downward scroll across the grid
- On-screen text: `Search. Filter. Browse with ease.`
- Voiceover: \"Customers can browse with search, filters, and beautifully organized product pages.\"

### Scene 4 - Product Detail

- Duration: 18 to 24 seconds
- Visual: product detail page with image, price, description, reviews
- Camera move: soft zoom on product hero, then drift downward toward reviews
- On-screen text: `Detailed pages that build trust`
- Voiceover: \"They can read reviews, build their cart with ease, and apply offers instantly.\"

### Scene 5 - Cart and Checkout

- Duration: 24 to 31 seconds
- Visual: cart drawer with quantity, coupon, totals, WhatsApp CTA
- Camera move: static hold with slight zoom
- On-screen text: `Checkout powered by WhatsApp`
- Voiceover: \"And when they are ready, the order moves smoothly into a simple WhatsApp handoff.\"

### Scene 6 - Closing Brand Frame

- Duration: 31 to 35 seconds
- Visual: return to homepage or hero crop with elegant fade
- Camera move: very slow push-in
- On-screen text: `Built for brands that want to sell with confidence`
- Voiceover: \"It is premium, clear, and made to help a beauty brand sell with confidence.\"

## Motion Direction

- use soft easing, not flashy movement
- prefer slow push-ins, light pans, and smooth vertical drift
- transitions should be dissolves or elegant slide blends
- avoid aggressive zooms or social-media gimmick transitions

## Music Direction

- warm, clean, premium instrumental
- light percussion, soft pads, subtle pulse
- no heavy beat drop
- the music should support trust and sophistication, not dominate the voiceover

## Voice Direction

- warm and composed
- feminine or neutral premium tone
- not overly dramatic
- calm confidence, clear diction, lightly paced
